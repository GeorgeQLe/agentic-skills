Business AFPS

The deliberate business route moves from uncertainty to production-ready work.

1. Concept and market

idea-scope-brief -> customer-discovery -> competitive-analysis -> journey-map -> positioning

2. Product shape

user-flow-map -> state-model optional -> ux-variations -> ui-interview

3. Prototype proof

build-ui-screens / logic-wiring -> uat -> consolidate-prototypes

4. Production handoff

research-roadmap --post-prototype -> spec-interview -> research-roadmap --post-spec

5. Execution

roadmap -> plan-phase -> exec -> ship -> ship-end
