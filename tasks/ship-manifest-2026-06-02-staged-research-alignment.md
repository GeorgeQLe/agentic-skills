## Residual Risk

- The staged workflow is a contract update rather than a live end-to-end research run. Tests verify contract text and routing, but they do not execute a real Stage 1 -> Stage 2 -> Stage 3 research invocation.
- The change is broad and generated-heavy. The generator dry-run and showcase validation reduce drift risk, but future skill-specific edge cases may need local wording refinements.
- Some `type: research` skills may be report-like rather than canonical-output-heavy; applying the shared staged section uniformly is intentional to avoid ambiguous pre-approval writes.
