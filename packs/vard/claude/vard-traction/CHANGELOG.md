# Changelog

## v0.1 - 2026-07-01

- Made `business-research` the canonical Business AFPS graduation pack and added the `npx skillpacks install business-research` prerequisite.
- Split the graduation entry route between `/idea-scope-brief` for raw/new product framing and `/customer-discovery` for shipped concepts with clear traction evidence.
- Expanded the evidence handoff to include VARD scan, align, ship-log, and traction-log entries.

## v0.0 - 2026-06-15

- Initial VARD traction check: compares a shipped app's post-launch signals (users, shares, engagement, revenue intent, inbound interest) against the VARD graduation thresholds and recommends iterate, graduate to Business AFPS, or archive. Acts as the semi-automatic graduation gate — recommends, user confirms.
